Built for people who get things done

A task manager that doesn't just organize your work — it does it.

Calendar & to-dos merged

Your schedule and your tasks live in one place. Plan by day and check things off without switching apps.

Lists

Group related items into focused lists. Keep projects, ideas, and recurring routines organized your way.

Books

Collect long-form notes and references into books — durable knowledge your agent can read and build on.

Lightweight agent

A fast, focused agent that stays out of your way. Minimal overhead, quick to spin up, easy to steer.

Portable sandbox

The agent runs in an isolated VM that travels with you — run it locally or in the cloud, same environment everywhere.

Multi-modal

Work across text, images, and files. Hand the agent whatever you've got and let it reason over all of it.
